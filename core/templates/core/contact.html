Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Campus Network?
Campus Network is a social platform designed exclusively for university students to connect, share updates, and stay informed about campus events, announcements, and departmental news. It's your digital campus community!
Who can join Campus Network?
Currently, Campus Network is open to all university students, faculty members, and staff with a valid university email address. We verify each registration to maintain a safe and authentic campus community.
Is Campus Network free to use?
Yes! Campus Network is completely free for all university students and faculty members. We believe in providing accessible communication tools for the entire campus community.

Account & Profile

How do I create an account?
Click on the "Register" button and fill in your details. You'll need your university email address for verification. After registration, you can set up your profile with your photo, department, and batch information.
How do I change my department or batch?
Go to your Profile Settings (click on your username in the top right, then "Settings"). There you can update your department, batch, and other profile information. Changes will be reflected immediately.
Can I delete my account?
Yes, you can delete your account from the Profile Settings page. Go to the "Danger Zone" section and click "Delete Account". Please note that this action is permanent and will remove all your posts, comments, and data from our platform.
I forgot my password. What should I do?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions to create a new password.

Posts & Content

What kind of content can I post?
You can share text posts, images, and engage with your campus community. Feel free to share updates, ask questions, discuss course materials, promote events, or just connect with fellow students. Just remember to keep it respectful and follow our community guidelines.
What are the privacy settings for posts?
When creating a post, you can choose from three privacy options:
  • Public: Visible to everyone on Campus Network
  • Followers Only: Visible only to your followers
  • Private: Visible only to you
Can I edit or delete my posts?
Yes! Click on the three dots menu on your post to access edit and delete options. You can edit your post content or delete it entirely. Edited posts will show an "(edited)" indicator.
How do comments and replies work?
You can comment on any post by typing in the comment box. To reply to a specific comment, click the "Reply" button under that comment. Replies are nested and indented for easy reading, creating threaded conversations.

Announcements & Departments

What are departmental announcements?
Departmental announcements are official notices, events, and news posted by department authorities. These can include exam schedules, class updates, departmental events, important deadlines, and other official communications.
Who can post announcements?
Only authorized users (department heads, faculty members, or designated student representatives) can create announcements. If you need posting privileges for your department, please contact the administration.
How do I see announcements from my department?
Make sure your profile has the correct department selected. Then visit the Explore page where you'll see announcements targeted to your department. You can also filter announcements by department using the sidebar filters.

Privacy & Safety

How is my data protected?
We take data protection seriously. All connections are encrypted using HTTPS, passwords are securely hashed, and we never share your personal information with third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for detailed information.
How do I report inappropriate content?
Click on the three dots menu on any post or comment and select "Report". Choose the reason for reporting, and our moderation team will review it promptly. You can also report users directly from their profile.
Can I block another user?
Yes, go to the user's profile, click on the three dots menu, and select "Block User". Blocked users cannot see your posts, follow you, or send you messages. You can unblock them anytime from your settings.

Technical Issues

I can't upload photos. What should I do?
Make sure your image is under 5MB for profile photos and 10MB for post images. Supported formats are JPG, PNG, and GIF. If you're still having issues, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.
Why am I not receiving notifications?
Check your browser settings to ensure notifications are enabled for Campus Network. Also verify your email settings in your profile. You can manage notification preferences in your account settings.
The site is loading slowly. What can I do?
Try clearing your browser cache, disabling browser extensions, or using an incognito/private window. If the problem persists, contact our support team with details about your browser and operating system.

Contact & Support

How can I contact support?
You can reach our support team through the Contact page, email us at support@campusnetwork.edu, or visit us at our campus office. We typically respond within 24 hours on weekdays.
I have a suggestion for improvement. Where can I share it?
We'd love to hear your suggestions! Use the Contact form or email us at feedback@campusnetwork.edu. Your input helps us make Campus Network better for everyone.
