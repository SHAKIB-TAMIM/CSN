Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Learn how we protect your data.

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

Introduction

Campus Network ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform. By using Campus Network, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

We prioritize your privacy and security. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register for Campus Network, we collect:

  • Name and username
  • Email address (university email required)
  • Student ID (optional, for verification)
  • Department and batch information
  • Profile photo and cover photo
  • Any other information you choose to add to your profile

1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use our platform:

  • Posts, comments, and interactions
  • Messages and conversations
  • Announcements viewed and engaged with
  • Login times and session duration
  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and operating system

1.3 Content You Share

Any content you post, share, or upload to Campus Network, including:

  • Posts and comments
  • Photos and images
  • Files and attachments
  • Stories and temporary content

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Personalize your experience
  • Connect you with your department and campus community
  • Send important notifications and announcements
  • Improve and optimize our platform
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in these limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you explicitly choose to share content publicly
  • With department admins: For verification and announcement purposes
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect rights
  • Service providers: Trusted third parties who assist in operating our platform

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Monitoring for suspicious activity

Your password is never stored in plain text. We use bcrypt hashing with salt.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request account deletion (see Section 8)
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Object: Opt-out of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@campusnetwork.edu.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze platform usage
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect platform functionality.

7. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Email delivery (for notifications)
  • Analytics (to improve our service)
  • File storage (for uploaded content)

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

8. Account Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • All your personal information is permanently removed
  • Posts and comments are deleted
  • Messages are removed
  • Profile information is erased

Some data may be retained for legal purposes or anonymous analytics, but will be disassociated from your identity.

Request Account Deletion

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • Email notification
  • Site-wide announcement
  • Notice at login

Continued use of Campus Network after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
